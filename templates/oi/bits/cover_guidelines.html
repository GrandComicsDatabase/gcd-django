Guidelines for cover uploads:
  • Please supply accurate scans that reflect the look of the actual cover.
  • Please do not upload
    • pre-production cover images, cover images used for solicitation, etc.
    • altered scans, scans with watermarks, or scans of the cover reprinted in other comics
    as these can be or are different from the actual printed cover.
  • If you upload a scan from another website, make sure you have permission to do that and mention the source.
  • A scan needs to be perfectly trimmed - no extra edges around - and not rotated in any way.
  • In regard to image sizes:
    • Images must be at least 400 pixels in width (for landscape covers in height), while 1000 pixels is preferred.
    • For wraparound and gatefold covers, the width limit applies to the front part, so the full scan must be at least 800 pixels wide, or possibly wider in some instances.
    • Do not blow up scans to meet this requirement as this results in muddy pictures.
  • Your username will be stored with the scan. Scan credits mostly do not show on the site, but will in the future.
  • If you accidentally upload the wrong cover you can discard your scan from your editing queue.
  • See also the covers FAQ.
