Reserve Ongoing Series

You may place an ongoing reservation on a series, which will cause all new issues added to that series to immediately be reserved to you. There is a limit on how many ongoing reservations an indexer can have at once. If you believe you can keep up with more series than the default limit, contact an editor about having your limit increased.
