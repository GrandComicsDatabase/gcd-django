This is a placeholder front page for accessing the inducks data. It is not, nor is it intended to be, particularly interesting.

You can access a list of issues for a particular series with urls of the form
http://maelstrom.walton.uark.edu/inducks/series/<country_code>/<inducks_series_id>/
For example http://maelstrom.walton.uark.edu/inducks/series/us/DD/

Or go directly to an issue by
http://maelstrom.walton.uark.edu/inducks/issue/<country_code>/<inducks_issue_id>/
and see the contents of the issue including its GCD flatfile format for cut-and-paste at the end.
For example http://maelstrom.walton.uark.edu/inducks/issue/no/DD2000-45/
